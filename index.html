My Family

I come from a large family that has eight people in total. My youngest sibling is my little sister, who is in fifth grade and can sometimes be very annoying. I am the second youngest, and my brother is the middle child. He is currently in college and can also be annoying at times. My Pets

I currently have six pets living in my house. These include one dog, one cat, and four chickens. I got my cat from a farm in Ramona, although I am not sure where my mom got our dog. The story of our chickens is a little more interesting. At first, we had six chickens, but as they grew older, we discovered that four of them were roosters, so we had to give them away because they were too loud. Later, a bobcat got into our backyard and took one of the remaining chickens, which was very sad. After that happened, my mom decided to get four more hens so the last chicken would not feel lonely.
